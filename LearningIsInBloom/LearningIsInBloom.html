Sample 2

Do you find that each year you have to blow the dust off of your “New Year’s Resolutions” list and recycle the same promises you made the year before? While we’re all hopeful that on January 1st we’ll magically lose enough weight to fit into skinnier jeans, crave nothing but leafy-green vegetables, and become financially savvy, the reality is, those “resolutions” usually resolve themselves by being pushed under a dusty rug.

Stop the cycle and resolve to make a true change and clear out old habits. Take a look at the past year and think: have you learned from your experiences and made an effort to improve? Whatever the answer, your New Year can start for the better by pledging to learn something new using the Learning Management System.

This savvy system offers:

  • Free online courses to all employees with topics ranging from leadership, communication, best business practices and more
  • Tracking and progress features to save courses for a later date and monitor success
  • Collaboration with your manager to create a career path through assigned training  

Celebrate a New Year and make a toast to learning something new and the never-ending quest to improve personally and professionally.
