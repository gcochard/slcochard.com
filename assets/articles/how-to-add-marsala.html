How to Add Marsala to Your Home

By Stephanie Cochard

The color authority, Pantone, named “Marsala” as the 2015 Color of the Year, and its no surprise that this earthy hue is bursting throughout the décor industry as an emerging must-have trend. Rooted in rich, red-brown tones that lend a natural rustic feel to any home, it’s easy to inject this crowd-pleasing palette into every room with a few simple touches. Here are 5 tips to be on trend with inspiration from this season’s new it-color:

1. Flower Vases. Nothing makes a home feel more inviting than fresh flowers, so look for a wine-colored vase that can be placed in any bright corner of your home to add instant life. Marsala is highly compatible with amber, golden yellows, greens in both turquoise and teal, and blues in the more vibrant range, so choose flowers that best compliment the hue to maximize the effect.

2. Wood Accents. Because of Marsala’s rustic earth tone, wood is an easy-to-find material that will seamlessly weave the color into your home. Invest in a small accent table, shelving, framing, or small knickknacks to effortlessly showcase the trend. Whether unfinished or in a variety of stains, wood objects are classic additions to any home that will also add a touch of modern charm.

3.Throw Pillows. Arguably there is no easier way to jazz up home décor than with throw pillows. They are quick to switch out, relatively inexpensive to replace, and with little commitment they make a big statement. Look for Marsala-inspired pillows for your couches and bedrooms to smoothly introduce a pop of color.

4. Kitchenware. Coffee mugs, bowls, plates, utensils and other smaller kitchen items are everyday ways to enjoy color.  Mix in Marsala with whites and other neutrals to set a table that is both elegant and visually interesting.

5. Wall Art. Fresh focal art pieces on your walls will instantly update your home’s look. Change out artwork in a highly trafficked area, like an entryway or near the kitchen, and look for geometric designs that feature Marsala to add a modern edge. For a longer-term commitment to the color, consider painting an accent wall for a bolder statement.

With its natural warmth and many possibilities, adding this 2015 Color of the Year is a simple and contemporary way to make any living space feel more inviting. With a few simple touches, you can help your home feel fresh and ready to take on the season in style.
