A 203K Loan Could Be Your Key to Renovation Rescue

Imagine you’ve found a home in your perfect neighborhood. It’s close to the playground that your kids love. It’s just around the corner from your favorite gym, which is important because you will definitely be getting fit for 2015. And it’s just a leap away from the farmer’s market that sells the best peaches on Sundays. The only problems are:

  • The kitchen needs updating (no sign of those granite countertops your heart desired).  
  • The roof is leaking (buckets are not attractive decorative items in your living room).
  • It doesn’t have that extra bathroom you need (the kids won’t share and you’ve heard the yelling to prove it).

Your solution? It might be an FHA 203K Home Loan. Intended for the purchase or refinance of properties that require minor repairs and upgrades, 203K loans are backed by the government and can allow you to buy the house you want and then provide the money you need to make repairs. It is a loan option that negates the need for two loans - one for the home and another for the repairs - saving you both time and money.

With 203K financing, you can buy an older home and fix it up, or you can purchase a short sale or foreclosure in less than move-in ready condition. You can also buy a property that is in good shape, but would be better with modernization and more energy efficiency. This option could allow you to find a home in your desired neighborhood and, through renovations, make it your perfect home in the right location.

The catch? Not all properties may qualify for this type of loan and not many lenders offer them. Discuss this option with a professional and get more information.
