There’s No Pants Like Jeans

We know jeans can’t compare to any other set of pantaloons in your closet, but it's time to drop the denim back into your drawers and resume standard business casual dress.

We’re not in summer anymore, Aunty Em. Your jeans were like your ruby slippers, guiding you along the yellow brick road of summer, making you feel comfortable and magical along the way.  

But even Dorothy goes home – back to black and white life in Kansas, to normal dress and to a post-Oz life. There are worse things, like getting carried away by flying monkeys or melting into a pool of green, witchy goop.

And no – it wasn’t all a dream. You were there and “Casual for a Cause” was there, raising over $100,000 through your generous donations to wounded veterans and their families. (If you ask us, you are a good witch.)

 You never know – we might wake up in Oz again sometime soon and skip down that “Casual for a Cause” road again. Until then, there’s no place like home.
